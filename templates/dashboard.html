About IN- RiskPrediction

This dashboard uses machine learning and geospatial analysis to predict disaster risks across India. It helps users make informed travel and safety decisions by analyzing historical data and environmental patterns.

Model Used: Random Forest Classifier (Sci-kit Learn)

Dataset: 10 years of simulated historical climatic and geographic data (2015-2025).
